Angela's Recipe For The Best
Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients...

Foe the dough:-

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teasppons yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 3 cup bread flour

For the filling:-

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons griund cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions...

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minuites
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic warp ,leave
    it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
